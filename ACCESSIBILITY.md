## Accessibility Commitment for SVL-KPA (Sanders Viopro Labs LLC)

We are dedicated to making our platform accessible to all users, in line with the Keep People Alive (KPA) mission. This document outlines our approach to accessibility.

### Standards
- WCAG 2.1 compliance
- Keyboard navigation and screen reader support

### Ongoing Improvements
- Regular accessibility audits
- User feedback and continuous improvement
