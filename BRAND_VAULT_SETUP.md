# Brand Vault Requirements & Setup Guide

### Part 4: Content Gaps & Opportunities

Search Atlas will show:

**Questions to Answer**:
- What keywords rank your competitors but NOT you?
- What volume keywords are unranked/low-ranking?
- What content topics are missing?
- Where's your biggest ranking opportunity?

**Document findings**:
```
Gap Analysis Template:

Keyword: [keyword]
Volume: [monthly searches]
Current Rank: [your position]
Competitor Rank: [competitor position]
Opportunity Level: High/Medium/Low
Recommended Content: [type of content needed]

Example:
Keyword: "crisis support app"
Volume: 1,200/month
Current Rank: Not ranking
Competitor Rank: Calm #3, BetterHelp #1
Opportunity: HIGH
Content: Landing page + blog posts
```

## 📊 Key Metrics to Track

**In Brand Vault, monitor**:

| Metric | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Visibility Score** | % of keyword volume you're capturing (target: >50%) |
| **Top 3 Rankings** | Keywords where you rank #1-3 (these drive traffic) |
| **Keywords Tracked** | Total keywords in your universe (target: 50-100) |
| **Avg Rank Position** | Where you rank on average (lower = better) |
| **Trend Direction** | Are rankings improving/declining over time? |
| **Competitive Share** | Your rank compared to competitors |

## 💡 Pro Tips for TokSEO Integration

**For best results with TokSEO**:

1. **Diverse Keywords**: 50+ keywords across all app features
   - Crisis support, wellness, AI, safety, health
   - Mix of transactional and informational

2. **Strong Competitor Set**: Track 5-8 competitors
   - Shows what rankings are possible
   - Identifies gaps TokSEO can target

3. **Local Keywords**: If serving specific regions
   - TokSEO can recommend location-specific strategies
   - Helps identify local expansion opportunities

4. **Content Themes**: Organize keywords by topic
   - Crisis support content strategy
   - Wellness & health content
   - Feature explainers
   - User success stories

5. **Monthly Reviews**: Analyze trends
   - TokSEO references this data in recommendations
   - Helps inform content calendar
