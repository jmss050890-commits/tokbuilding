# Portable Hydroponic System Integration Plan for Guardians (KPA Mission)

## 1. Overview
Integrate a compact hydroponic system with guardian units to provide fresh food, education, and resilience for KPA (Keep People Alive) initiatives.

## 2. Recommended System
- **Type:** Deep Water Culture (DWC) or Nutrient Film Technique (NFT)
- **Size:** Portable (tabletop or backpack-sized)
- **Materials:** Food-safe plastics, non-toxic components
- **Power:** Low-voltage pump (USB, battery, or solar)
- **Plants:** Leafy greens, herbs, fast-growing vegetables

## 3. Digital Guardian Integration
- **Sensors:** Water level, pH, nutrient, and temperature (optional, for advanced monitoring)
- **Connectivity:** Bluetooth/Wi-Fi for alerts and data logging (optional)
- **Interface:**
  - Setup wizard and video guides
  - Real-time status and reminders
  - Troubleshooting assistant
  - Educational content (nutrition, sustainability)

## 4. Safety & Maintenance
- Use only food-safe, BPA-free plastics
- Provide clear cleaning and maintenance instructions
- Include water overflow and electrical safety warnings
- Recommend child-safe designs (no sharp edges, secure lids)

## 5. Example Kits
- General Hydroponics Waterfarm (DWC)
- AeroGarden Sprout (tabletop, plug-and-play)
- DIY: 5-gallon bucket DWC with air pump

## 6. Next Steps
- Select kit or design for your deployment
- Prototype integration with digital guardian interface
- Test with target users and gather feedback
- Iterate and scale
