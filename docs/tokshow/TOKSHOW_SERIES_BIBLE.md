# TokShow Series Bible

Brand: Sanders Viopro Labs LLC (SVL)
Mission Standard: KPA - Keep People Alive
Series Type: Realistic cartoon (family-safe, purpose-driven)
Launch Date: April 5, 2026 (Easter Sunday)

## Core Premise
TokShow follows Kysen and his little sister Bam Bam as they solve real neighborhood problems through teamwork, courage, and practical safety leadership. Each episode delivers one clear KPA takeaway viewers can use in real life.

## Tone and Style
- Visual tone: grounded and realistic with warm color accents and expressive, cinematic framing
- Emotional tone: hopeful, honest, funny at times, never careless about safety
- Story pace: 10-14 minute episodes, three-act structure, clear stakes, practical resolution
- Audience: family and community viewers

## Main Characters

### Kysen
- Role: heart-first strategist
- Strengths: observation, empathy, planning, faith-based courage
- Growth arc: moves from self-doubt to steady leadership under pressure

### Bam Bam
- Role: fast-action protector
- Family role: Kysen's little sister
- Strengths: speed, loyalty, humor, decisive support
- Growth arc: learns that protection includes planning, not just reaction

## KPA Episode Framework (Required)
1. Trigger: a realistic safety challenge appears
2. Response: Kysen and Bam Bam gather facts and protect people first
3. Action: they coordinate practical community help
4. Outcome: crisis reduced, people safer
5. Takeaway: one repeatable KPA practice for viewers

## Brand and Production Rules
- Keep SVL brand visible in opening slate and end card
- Keep KPA mission line in every episode close
- Avoid glamorizing danger or reckless behavior
- Prioritize realistic, teachable safety behaviors
- End each episode with: "KPA Action Step"

## Episode Runtime Template
- Intro (0:00-1:00)
- Problem Setup (1:00-4:00)
- Strategy and Action (4:00-10:00)
- Resolution and KPA Action Step (10:00-12:00)
