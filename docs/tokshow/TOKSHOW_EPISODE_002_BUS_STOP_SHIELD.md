# TokShow Episode 02
Title: The Bus Stop Shield
Series: TokShow
Brand: Sanders Viopro Labs LLC
Mission: KPA - Keep People Alive

## Logline
On a chaotic school morning, Kysen and Bam Bam build a practical bus stop protection system so younger children are never left unmonitored.

## Three-Act Structure

### Act 1 - The Bus Stop Risk
- Monday rush creates unsafe wait conditions
- Younger kids are exposed during staggered arrivals
- Near-miss crossings trigger immediate action

### Act 2 - The Shield Plan
- Two-zone layout: child lane and parent lane
- Rotating adult watch with time-based handoff
- Verbal check-in protocol for every child transfer

### Act 3 - Safe Departures
- Boarding becomes calm and consistent
- Late pickup handled through backup contact flow
- Neighborhood adopts the Bus Stop Shield plan

## Sample Dialogue
Kysen: "Structure beats stress. We set the lanes first."
Bam Bam: "No kid waits unseen."
Kysen: "Say the name, confirm the face, log the move."
Bam Bam: "Bus stop shield is up."

## KPA Action Step
Build a Bus Stop Shield Card before next school day:
1. Mark one safe waiting zone
2. Assign one adult handoff lead
3. Use one verbal check-in rule for every child
4. Post one backup contact list in the lane
