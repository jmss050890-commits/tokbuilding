# TokShow Episode 01
Title: Easter Sunday: The Promise Route
Series: TokShow
Brand: Sanders Viopro Labs LLC
Mission: KPA - Keep People Alive
Premiere: April 5, 2026 (Easter Sunday)

## Logline
After Easter service, a sudden storm knocks out streetlights on a key walk-home route. Kysen and Bam Bam organize a neighborhood safety path that gets elders, kids, and families home safely.

## Three-Act Structure

### Act 1 - The Disruption
- Opening: church bells, family greetings, bright Sunday mood
- Trigger: heavy rain and power loss darken two blocks
- Stakes: children and elders need to cross dim streets

### Act 2 - The Plan
- Kysen maps a temporary safe route using porch lights and known houses
- Bam Bam recruits volunteers for corner watch and umbrella escorts
- They divide tasks:
  - check elders first
  - group children in pairs
  - keep everyone on one lit route

### Act 3 - The Protection Win
- Route opens with volunteer checkpoints
- A tense crossing moment is resolved through calm communication
- Everyone reaches home or family pickup safely
- Final image: sunrise breaking through clouds as Kysen and Bam Bam post a route sign for future drills

## Sample Script Beats

### Scene 1
Kysen: "Bam Bam, no panic. We protect first, then we move."
Bam Bam: "Copy that. I'm on corner teams now."

### Scene 2
Kysen (to neighbors): "We only need three things: light, clear lanes, and check-ins."
Neighbor: "I'm covering the first corner."

### Scene 3
Bam Bam: "Kids in pairs. Elders first. We walk together."
Kysen: "KPA means nobody gets left behind."

### Final Scene
Narrator: "When the lights went out, the neighborhood lit up."
Kysen: "Same plan next time, faster."
Bam Bam: "KPA every day."
