Mission: KPA - Keep People Alive

## Opportunity

The SVL Portable Hydroponic Garden System is a compact, practical food-resilience product designed for households and community environments where consistent access to fresh food is limited.

## Why This Matters

- Strengthens daily nutrition access
- Supports resilience for families and communities
- Creates practical education and engagement opportunities
- Aligns with mission-driven business growth

## What Is Built Today

- Product architecture and integration plan
- User guide and deployment workflow
- AI ecosystem support through SVL Guardian infrastructure
- Blueprint package framework with engineering handoff templates

## Commercial Paths

1. Residential direct-to-consumer starter kits
2. Community program deployments (schools/churches/nonprofits)
3. Multifamily pilot programs with resident support model
4. White-labeled/branded partner editions

## Pilot Proposal (90 Days)

- Cohort size: 25 to 100 units
- Pilot objectives:
  - Setup completion rate
  - Grow success and retention
  - User satisfaction and repeat engagement
  - Unit economics and expansion readiness
