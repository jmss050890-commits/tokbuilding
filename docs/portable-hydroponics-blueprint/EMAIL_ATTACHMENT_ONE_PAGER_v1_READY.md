# TokStrategy One-Pager: SVL Portable Hydroponic Garden System

Organization: Sanders Viopro Labs LLC  
Mission: KPA - Keep People Alive

## Executive Opportunity

SVL is launching a portable hydroponic system designed for practical food resilience in homes, multifamily communities, and mission-driven programs. The system is engineered for simple setup, low-space use, and measurable outcomes aligned with KPA.

## Why This Is Strategic

- Converts wellness intent into daily food access behavior
- Supports resident retention and engagement when deployed in communities
- Enables a practical bridge between digital guidance and physical life support
- Creates scalable product lanes for direct and enterprise channels

## What Is Ready Now

- Core architecture and product definition
- Filled BOM draft with cost and supplier structure
- Drawing package index and reference drawings
- Validation protocol framework for pilot sign-off
- Blueprint package compiled for partner review

## Pilot Model (90 Days)

- Cohort sizes: 25-unit pilot then 100-unit expansion
- Target environment: Multifamily + community partners
- Pilot KPIs:
  - setup success >=90%
  - weekly care compliance >=80%
  - crop survival >=85%
  - user satisfaction >=4.4/5

## Commercial Paths

1. Residential starter kit distribution
2. Community resilience deployments (education/nonprofit)
3. Multifamily resident wellness bundle
4. Co-branded partner edition and managed services
