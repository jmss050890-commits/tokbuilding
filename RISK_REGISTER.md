# RISK_REGISTER.md

## Risk Register for Sanders Viopro Labs LLC (SVL-KPA)

This register tracks key risks, mitigations, and responsible parties for privacy, security, accessibility, and compliance.

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation | Owner |
|------|--------|------------|------------|-------|
| Data breach | High | Medium | Encryption, IRP, access controls | Security Lead |
| Accessibility gaps | Medium | Low | WCAG audits, user feedback | Accessibility Lead |
| Regulatory non-compliance | High | Low | Regular policy review, legal counsel | DPO |
| Third-party dependency | Medium | Medium | License review, vendor assessment | Engineering Lead |

---

*Update this register as new risks are identified or mitigated.*
