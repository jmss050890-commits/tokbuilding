# SVL Search Atlas 30-Day Execution Board

Timestamp: 2026-04-03 06:00:00 -05:00
Program: SVL-KPA Advantage
Owner: TokSEO / SVL Lab

## Week 1: Foundation and Technical Control

Day 1
- Connect Search Atlas project to primary domain.
- Verify tracking integrations (GSC + analytics).
- Confirm sitemap and robots visibility.

Day 2
- Run full technical audit.
- Fix all critical/high issues first.
- Log before/after status.

Day 3
- Finalize priority keyword clusters.
- Map each cluster to one target URL.

Day 4
- Baseline report: rankings, traffic, index count.
- Document top 10 opportunities.

Day 5
- Publish first optimization sprint (titles, metas, internal links).

## Week 2: Content Expansion and Internal Linking

- Refresh 3 high-value pages with intent-matched copy.
- Publish 2 new mission + product pages.
- Add strategic links from hub pages to conversion pages.
- Validate schema and metadata coverage.

## Week 3: Authority and Competitive Positioning

- Launch outreach/backlink sprint.
- Build citations for branded trust.
- Track competitor movement and update gaps.
- Refresh weak pages losing rankings.

## Week 4: Conversion Lift and Standardization

- Improve CTA clarity on top SEO landing pages.
- A/B test headline + CTA pair on one high-traffic page.
- Document wins, misses, and next 30-day priorities.

## Required Weekly Outputs

1. KPI snapshot
- sessions (organic)
- rankings (priority terms)
- indexed pages
- conversion rate from organic pages

2. Technical status
- critical/high issue counts
- crawl/index anomalies

3. Content output
- pages updated
- pages published
- internal links added

4. Authority output
- outreach sent
- links acquired

## Done Definition (30 Days)

- Technical criticals are zero.
- Priority keyword map is complete and active.
- Minimum 8 pages improved/published.
- Mission-aligned messaging maintained on all updated pages.
- Weekly reports saved under SVL folder.
