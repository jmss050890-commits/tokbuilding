### 3️⃣ HANDLE CRISES (When emergencies happen)

**These come in as RED ALERTS**

**Scenario 3: Suicide/Mental Health Crisis**

```
RED ALERT: "Derek called Grace Coach reporting suicidal thoughts"

KPA AI shows recommended actions:
☐ Confirm he has immediate safety support
☐ Connect with 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
☐ Check if he wants emergency services involved
☐ Follow up in 1 hour

You see his profile → Emergency contact → Support history

What you do:
1. Call Derek: "Hey, I see you reached out to Grace. Are you safe right now?"
2. Listen. Don't judge.
3. Use the script provided: "I want to make sure you get help. I'm going to..."
4. Execute one of the options:
   - Connect with 988 (KPA can call with him)
   - Emergency services (police/ambulance)
   - Emergency contact (family)
5. Stay on line until he's safe
6. Document what happened

KPA auto-logs everything. You just click "Resolved" or "Follow-up scheduled"
```

**Scenario 4: Domestic Violence Crisis**

```
RED ALERT: "Angela checked in and mentioned unsafe partner"

KPA AI recommends:
☐ Confidential conversation (no roommates nearby)
☐ Connect with National DV Hotline (1-800-799-7233)
☐ Safety planning
☐ Emergency contact safety verification

What you do:
1. Private call with Angela
2. "I'm glad you told me. You're not alone. There are people trained to help."
3. Read the de-escalation script (in dashboard)
4. Offer: "Do you want to talk to the DV hotline? I'll be right here."
5. Document her response
6. Schedule follow-up (tomorrow, 1 week, whatever is safest)
```

**Scenario 5: Medical Emergency**

```
RED ALERT: "James reported chest pain"

KPA AI recommends:
☐ Immediate medical evaluation (911)
☐ Confirm emergency services dispatched
☐ Notify emergency contact
☐ Document response time

What you do:
1. Call 911: "Resident at [address] reporting chest pain"
2. Call James: "Ambulance is on the way. Stay on the line with me."
3. Notify his emergency contact
4. Follow ambulance to hospital or wait for response
5. Document everything (times, actions, outcomes)

KPA tracks response time → Later reviewed for continuous improvement
```
