# DATA_GOVERNANCE.md

## Data Governance Policy for Sanders Viopro Labs LLC (SVL-KPA)

This document unifies our approach to privacy, security, accessibility, and community integrity, ensuring compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, CCPA, ISO/IEC 27001, and WCAG 2.1+ standards.

### Governance Principles
- Privacy by Design & Security by Design
- Regulatory alignment (GDPR, HIPAA, CCPA, ISO 27001)
- Accessibility (WCAG 2.1/2.2 compliance)
- Community integrity and DEI
