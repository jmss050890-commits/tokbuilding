# CARDONE KPA PILOT - ONE PAGE

Project: KPA-CARDONE-FPL-001
Prepared by: Jerome Sanders, Sanders Viopro Labs LLC
Purpose: Launch a controlled pilot in one Cardone-managed multifamily asset

## Pilot Goal
Deploy a practical Keep People Alive (KPA) layer for resident safety, crisis support, and response coordination, then measure outcomes in 60-90 days.

## Pilot Scope
- 1 property
- 60-90 day pilot window
- Property manager and designated staff onboarding
- Resident-facing safety pathways with clear escalation logic

## Commercial Structure (Pilot)
- Paid implementation fee at kickoff
- Monthly pilot management fee during test window
- Optional paid training day for property staff
- Pre-agreed scale pricing if KPI targets are met

## Commercial Structure (Suggested Range)
- Pilot setup and implementation: $15,000-$35,000 one-time
- Pilot management and reporting: $4,000-$9,000 per month
- Optional on-site training day: $3,500-$7,500
- Expansion pricing: finalized after KPI validation at Day 60 or 90

## What SVL Delivers
1. KPA operating framework and rollout plan
2. Resident safety flow and check-in pathways
3. Crisis support structure and escalation protocol
4. Weekly operating summary and executive dashboard
5. End-of-pilot report with scale recommendation

## What Cardone Team Provides
1. Pilot property selection
2. On-site point of contact
3. Access for onboarding and communications
4. KPI alignment on what success means

## Success Metrics (Initial)
- Resident safety engagement rate
- Check-in completion rate
- Escalation response timeliness
- Property team adoption and usability score
- Qualitative resident confidence feedback

## Decision Gates
- Day 14: Adoption checkpoint
- Day 30: Midpoint performance readout
- Day 60 or 90: Executive scale decision

## Scale Path If Pilot Performs
- Phase 2: Expand to 3-5 properties
- Phase 3: Portfolio rollout plan by market priority

## Why This Works
This pilot is designed for real operations: low friction onboarding, measurable outcomes, and fast executive visibility.

This structure also protects speed to revenue while preserving a clean path to portfolio expansion.

## Respectful Process Note
We respect Cardone team process and approval structure. Scope and pricing can be right-sized to fit property realities while protecting execution quality and measurable outcomes.
